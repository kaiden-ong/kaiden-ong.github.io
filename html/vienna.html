Internship Project in Austria

// designing taxonomies and ontologies & UIUX

Why in Vienna?

I travelled to Vienna, Austria in the summer of 2023, to work for Semantic Web Company, who are headquartered there. As a leader market leader for semantic solutions, their software PoolParty leverages machine learning, NLP and graph technologies to create deep and comprehensive models within a domain. Taxonomies, ontologies, and knowledge graphs are great for holding this kind of information as they help provide semantic meaning behind words and phrases, in a simple yet effective manner. I worked with one of their clients, who specializes in the agricultural research field and had the privilege of helping them:

Redesigning Thesaurus Website
Revamping Taxonomy & Ontology
Resarch SPARQL Queries for Search Results
Provide Feedback & Recommendations

How I Got Here

As part of a study abroad with the iSchool (Information School) at UW, I has selected to go on a study abroad with several other students to work with Semantic Web Company (SWC). From four of SWC's clients, I chose to work with an international non-profit organization that provides agricultural information to people around the world, whether that's easily applcable knowledge, reserach knowledge, or anything in between. I worked with two other students from UW and over the course of this trip, we redesigned the client's UIUX and helped them review and provide future suggestions for their PoolParty architecture.
