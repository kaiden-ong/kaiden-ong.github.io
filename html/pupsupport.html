What is PupSupport

PupSupport is a community-driven initiative that utilizes art and design to promote mental health awareness and equip college students with essential skills for sustaining and improving their mental well-being. But they also needed a website. So our dedicated team of three designers and six coders collaborated with PupSupport to create an innovative and user-friendly website to break the barriers that college students face with their mental health. Over the course of two quarters in the 2022 - 2023 school year, we designed and implemented a website with:

Interactive Website Design
Embedded Spotify Podcasts
Javascript Powered Functions
Feedback and Questions Form

How it began

Web Impact (formerly known as Dubvelopers) is a University of Washington RSO/club. With Web Impact, I had the opportunity to work with others to create a website for underrepresented clients who service local communities or businesses. Throughout the fall quarter, I attended both design and coding workshops to sharpen my skills, as I hoped to land a position on one of the teams. As the end of fall quarter approached we were tasked with creating a personal project as part of our application. I decided to create something that was useful to me at home, while still showcasing my HTML/CSS and especially JavaScript skills, a chess timer to make my games with my younger brother faster and more exciting (click here for the Github repository). I worked on this project tirelessly, both designing and coding, throughout winter break, but it was all worth it when I was accepted onto a team!
