Heartbeats App

// spreading kindness through social media

How can an app create kindness?

We embarked on this project with a vision to cultivate kindness in daily life. Through our app, we empower users with daily prompts and challenges to spread kindness, fostering a global community of empathy and compassion. By leveraging location tracking technology, users can visualize the ripple effect of their actions, contributing to a more interconnected and caring world. Throughout this project, I honed my skills in:

Kotlin Programming
Firebase Authentication and Databases
Integrating Third-Party APIs
Agile Development

Staying the Course

Having recently learned Kotlin and Android development skills, our team had come up with a novel and fun app idea. Drawing inspiration from popular social media platforms like Instagram, Twitter, and BeReal, we envisioned a space that would diverge from the toxic environments often found on these platforms and instead cultivate a community of kind and caring individuals. We set and accomplished our goals to create features such daily challenges, location-based posts, leaderboards, privacy options, user authentication, and a friend/following system. While the journey was certainly not a simple one, the sense of accomplishment upon completion made it all the more rewarding.

Timeline

① Brainstorming

As new Android developer, we wanted to challenge ourselves in creating a full stack app leaving us plenty of room to make mistakes and learn more.At the same time, we wanted to have fun and create something brand new, eventually landing us with our app "Heartbeats".

② The Brick Wall

For several weeks, we tried a MongoDB implementation, but faced recurring errors, dampening our motivation and progress. Together, we persevered, seeking alternative solutions and eventually landing on Firebase.

③ Hard at Work

Over the course of many weeks, we began to conquer challenges one feature at a time, adopting a vertical development approach. One feature at a time, we implemented, debugged, and reviewed until we finally ended with a project that we were all extremely proud of.

④ The Final Boss

Finally, we had the opportunity to showcase our app, presenting it to not only other students but also to a PhD candidate in search of Android developers, and three Android professionals from the industry. Their enthusiastic response validated our dedication, as our app received praise from both peers and seasoned experts.

An Unmatched Experience

This project has left me deeply satisfied and immensely proud. Each step presented challenges, pushing me to grow as a developer. Despite setbacks, the sense of accomplishment upon overcoming obstacles was unparalleled. Collaborating with a dedicated team and seeing our vision come to life has been truly inspiring. This project stands as a testament to our hard work, perseverance, and commitment to excellence. I am incredibly proud of our achievement and look forward to applying the lessons learned to future endeavors.
